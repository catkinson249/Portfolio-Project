About Us

Mission

Our mission is to bring you the tastiest treats that will remind you of those made by your grandma. We also want offer a style of desserts that Is not considered “gourmet”.

Opportunities

We always are looking for new grandmas to showcase their cooking skills. Here, you make your own hours and earn cash for each item(s) cooked.

Success

Our success comes from our grandmas and customers. We stand out from other dessert companies simply by bringing our customers the warm fuzzy feeling they knew as a child. No gourmet styles, no oddball flavors, nothing but love is all we promise.
